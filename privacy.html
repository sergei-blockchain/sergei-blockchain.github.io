Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 8, 2026

Welcome to FamilyNotes. Your privacy is not just a feature; it is the core of our architecture. This policy explains how we handle your data.

1. Zero-Knowledge & End-to-End Encryption

FamilyNotes is built on a "Zero-Knowledge" architecture. This means:

  • Content Encryption: Your notes, photos, and checklists are encrypted on your device using AES-256 and Elliptic Curve Cryptography (P-256) before being sent to our servers.
  • No Access: We do not hold your encryption keys. We cannot read your notes, view your photos, or listen to your messages. If you lose your keys, we cannot recover your data.

2. Data We Collect

We only collect the minimum information necessary to provide our service:

  • Account Information: Your email address and a unique User ID (provided via Firebase Auth) to sync your encrypted data across devices.
  • Metadata: Non-sensitive identifiers such as Family IDs and timestamps to manage synchronization and permissions.
  • Subscription Data: We use Apple’s StoreKit 2 to process subscriptions. We receive confirmation of your active subscription but do not see your credit card details.

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following trusted providers:

  • Google Firebase: For secure data storage (Firestore) and authentication.
  • Apple: For payment processing and secure iCloud/Keychain sync.

4. Data Deletion

You have full control over your data. You can delete your account and all associated encrypted records directly from the app settings at any time. Once deleted, this data is unrecoverable.

5. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect the integrity of your encrypted data packets. However, the ultimate security depends on the safety of your personal device.
